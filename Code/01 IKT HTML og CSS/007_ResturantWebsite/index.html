About Us

At Velvet Whisker, we believe that animals deserve a dining experience that is as elegant and delightful as it is for their human companions. Situated in the heart of Easter Island, our restaurant is a haven for animal lovers, providing a unique and enchanting environment where pets and their owners can share memorable culinary moments.

With a deep appreciation for the bond between humans and animals, we have created Velvet Whisker to be a safe and inviting space for all creatures great and small. Our utmost commitment is to offer the highest quality of service and delectable dishes that satisfy both the discerning palates of animals and the expectations of their human counterparts.

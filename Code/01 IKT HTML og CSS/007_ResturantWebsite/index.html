About Us

At Velvet Whisker, we believe that animals deserve a dining experience that is as elegant and delightful as it is for their human companions. Situated in the heart of Easter Island, our restaurant is a haven for animal lovers, providing a unique and enchanting environment where pets and their owners can share memorable culinary moments.

With a deep appreciation for the bond between humans and animals, we have created Velvet Whisker to be a safe and inviting space for all creatures great and small. Our utmost commitment is to offer the highest quality of service and delectable dishes that satisfy both the discerning palates of animals and the expectations of their human counterparts.

Our Ambience

Step into a cozy and inviting atmosphere at Velvet Whisker. Our restaurant's interior is thoughtfully designed to create a relaxing environment for both pets and their human companions. Soft lighting, comfortable seating, and soothing background music ensure a stress-free and enjoyable dining experience for all.


Events and Special Services

In addition to our exceptional dining offerings, we also host a variety of events and provide special services to make your pet's visit memorable. Look out for:



  • Birthday Pawties: Celebrate your pet's special day with a customized menu and delightful surprises.
  • Training Workshops: Join our expert trainers for valuable tips and tricks to enhance your pet's obedience and manners.
  • Pet Pampering: Treat your furry friend to a luxurious grooming session or a relaxing pet massage in our dedicated spa area.
